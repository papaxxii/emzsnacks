What Is E&M'z Minute Snacks?

E&M'z Minute Snacks is a local snack delivery service showcasing Filipino street snacks and homemade treats.

Order popular local snacks and have them delivered to your location quickly. We support local producers and small food vendors.

Find banana chips, chicharon, otap, kwek-kwek, fishballs and more — all in one place.
